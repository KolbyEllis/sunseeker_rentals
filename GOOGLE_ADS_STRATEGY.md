## ‚öôÔ∏è BIDDING STRATEGY

### Phase 1: Weeks 1-2 (Learning Phase)
- **Strategy**: Manual CPC
- **Bid**: Start at $2.00-$3.50 per click (based on city)
- **Goal**: Gather conversion data
- **Action**: Monitor search terms, adjust bids daily

### Phase 2: Weeks 3-4 (Optimization Phase)
- **Strategy**: Target CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)
- **Target CPA**: $100-$150 per lead (based on $3k/year customer value)
  - **Math**: $3k customer value √∑ 20% conversion rate = $600 max CPA
  - **Target**: $100-$150 per lead = 20-30x ROI (conservative target)
  - **Aggressive**: Can go up to $200-$300 per lead if conversion rate is high
- **Goal**: Optimize for conversions
- **Action**: Let Google optimize, review daily

### Phase 3: Month 2+ (Automated Phase)
- **Strategy**: Maximize Conversions (within budget)
- **Target**: 12-20 leads per month total (3-5 customers at 20-25% close rate)
- **Goal**: Consistent lead flow
- **Action**: Weekly review, adjust budgets as needed

## üí° PRO TIPS FOR EXPLOSIVE PERFORMANCE

### 1. Landing Page Optimization
- **Critical**: Ensure each city page loads fast (under 3 seconds)
- **CTA Placement**: Make "Get Started Today" button above fold
- **Trust Signals**: Display review count and rating prominently
- **Mobile Optimization**: 80%+ of clicks will be mobile

### 2. Ad Copy Testing Strategy
- Test 3 ad variations per ad group
- Rotate evenly for first 2 weeks
- Pause lowest performer
- Create new variation to replace paused ad
- Continuous improvement cycle

### 3. Keyword Match Type Strategy
- **Start Broad**: Use phrase match initially to discover new terms
- **Scale Exact**: Move converting terms to exact match
- **Optimize Negative**: Continuously add irrelevant search terms

### 4. Conversion Tracking Setup
- Track form submissions on city pages
- Track phone calls (extensions + call tracking)
- Track Calendly bookings
- Set up Google Analytics goals
- Import conversions to Google Ads

### 5. Competitive Advantage Leveraging
- Highlight "28+ years" (most competitors don't have this)
- Emphasize "450+ properties" (proves scale)
- Use "165 Google reviews" (social proof)
- Mention "Family-owned" (differentiation)
