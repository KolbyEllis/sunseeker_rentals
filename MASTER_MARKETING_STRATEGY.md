## üö® GOOGLE ADS: CRITICAL ISSUES & FIXES

### **Current Campaign Problems:**

**Performance Max Campaign Issues:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **No Keywords to Set:** Unlike Search campaigns where you choose keywords, Performance Max doesn't use keywords. Google automatically picks which searches trigger your ads based on your assets (headlines, descriptions, images) and landing page.
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Google Chooses Search Matches:** Google uses machine learning to match your ads to searches it thinks are relevant, which is why you see such a wide variety of search terms - Google is trying to find any search it thinks might relate to your business.
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Too Broad Targeting:** Performance Max is showing ads on searches that are too broad and include many tenant searches (people looking to rent), not just property owners
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Irrelevant Search Matches:** Ads appearing on tenant searches like "houses for rent", "apartments for rent", "apartments under $1000 near me", "new house for rent near me"
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Wrong Service Matches:** Ads matching to irrelevant searches like "rental realtors", "vacation rental", "commercial property management", "best free pm software"
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Hidden Data:** 85.6% of clicks (4,826 out of 5,641) are from searches you can't see in the report
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Limited Control:** Performance Max automatically matches ads across Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail, etc. without giving you control over which searches trigger ads (you can only block searches with negative keywords)

**Search Terms Analysis (From Report):**
- **Total Campaign Performance:** 5,640 clicks, $2,937 spent
- **Visible Search Terms:** 815 clicks, $561 spent on searches you can see
- **Hidden Search Terms:** 4,826 clicks, $2,376 spent on searches Google doesn't show you
- **Relevant Searches (In Visible Terms):** Some good matches like "arizona property management", "property management services", "az prime property management" - showing Performance Max CAN find relevant searches
- **Problem:** Also matching to many tenant searches (people looking to rent, not manage properties) which dilutes targeting
- **CTR:** 1.20% (could be higher with better targeting)
- **CPC:** $0.52 (relatively low, but some clicks are low-value tenant searches)
