## üéØ CURRENT PERFORMANCE DATA (Baseline Metrics)

### Website Analytics (Google Analytics)
- [ ] **Current organic rankings** for each city (top 20 keywords per city)
  - Example: "Goodyear property management" = Position 5
  - Example: "Glendale property manager" = Position 8
- [ ] **Organic traffic** per city page (last 30, 90 days)
- [ ] **Bounce rate** per city page
- [ ] **Average time on page** per city page
- [ ] **Conversion rate** per city page (form submissions, phone calls)
- [ ] **Top landing pages** and their performance
- [ ] **Exit rate** for each city page

### Google Search Console Data
- [ ] **Click-through rate (CTR)** for each city keyword
- [ ] **Average position** for all city-related keywords
- [ ] **Impressions** vs **Clicks** for each city
- [ ] **Top queries** bringing traffic to each city page
- [ ] **Search appearance** issues or warnings
- [ ] **Core Web Vitals** scores (page speed, interactivity, visual stability)

### Current Google Ads Performance (If Running)
- [ ] **Current CPC** (Cost Per Click) by city
- [ ] **Current CTR** (Click-Through Rate) by city
- [ ] **Current conversion rate** by city
- [ ] **Current CPA** (Cost Per Acquisition) by city
- [ ] **Quality scores** for each ad group
- [ ] **Search term reports** (what people actually search)
- [ ] **Impression share** (how often your ads show)
- [ ] **Lost impression share** due to budget vs rank

## üí° HOW THIS DATA IMPROVES YOUR STRATEGY

### For Google Ads:
- **Better keyword targeting**: Know what people actually search
- **Better ad copy**: Match what converts vs what doesn't
- **Better budgets**: Allocate more to high-converting cities
- **Better bidding**: Know what CPC you can afford
- **Better landing pages**: Know what works vs what doesn't

### For Organic SEO (#1 Rankings):
- **Content gaps**: Know what competitors rank for that you don't
- **Keyword opportunities**: Find low-competition, high-value keywords
- **Technical issues**: Fix what's hurting your rankings
- **Link building**: Know where competitors get links
- **Local SEO**: Improve GMB and local citations
